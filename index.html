About Our Clinic

The Anxiety Clinic of Tallahassee is an outpatient clinic directed by Dr. Alexandria Meyer, a psychology professor at Florida State University. We specialize in treating anxiety disorders in children and families using evidence‑based approaches. Our goal is to provide compassionate, individualized care that empowers clients to overcome anxiety and improve quality of life.

Types of Anxiety

Anxiety and behavioral problems can manifest in many ways. Recognizing the type of anxiety your child is experiencing helps guide effective treatment.

Social Fears

Shyness, difficulty making friends, fear of being judged or speaking in front of others.

Specific Phobias

Intense fear of specific things such as the dark, heights, insects, doctors or storms.

Separation Fears

Fear of getting lost, difficulty separating from caregivers, refusal to sleep alone or go to school.

Obsessive‑Compulsive Thoughts

Repeating rituals, intrusive thoughts, worry about germs or the need for things to be “just right.”

Generalized Worries

Persistent worry about school, health, safety or the future, difficulty relaxing, seeking constant reassurance.

Panic & Trauma

Sudden panic attacks or reactions to traumatic events that result in avoidance, irritability or nightmares.

Therapy Approaches

Evidence‑based interventions tailored to your needs.

Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT)

A collaborative therapy focusing on identifying and changing thought patterns and behaviors that contribute to anxiety.

Parent‑Child Interaction Therapy (PCIT)

Strengthening parent–child relationships through live coaching to improve emotional regulation and reduce anxiety.

Exposure Therapy

Gradual exposure to feared situations or objects in a safe environment helps clients build confidence and resilience.

Our Approach

We use a structured, evidence‑based methodology that begins with assessment and builds towards confidence and independence.

Assessment & Education

We start with a comprehensive assessment of symptoms, triggers and history. We teach families about anxiety and how thoughts and behaviors can increase or maintain it.

Skills & Exposure

Using CBT and PCIT, we help clients identify and reframe maladaptive thoughts while gradually facing fears through exposure to build resilience.

Individualized Plan

We tailor every treatment plan to the needs of each child and family, integrating mindfulness, acceptance and family‑systems strategies when appropriate.
